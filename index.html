About Us

ORGANISATION PROFILE

Hisia Youth Guiding and Counselling C.B.O is a non-profit, community-based organization with active social footprint in Likoni sub-county. Hisia is legally registered with the ministry of labor and social protection under the department of social development. It aims at guiding youth in likoni and other parts of Mombasa.

Services

What We Do

We are a CBO that is championing for empowerment through counselling the community on matters Mental Health, GBV, SRHR and Drug and substance abuse.

Uchaguzi Bila Balaa Program

We hosted local administrators, youth leaders and social media personnel from Likoni Subcounty for the Inception meeting of the UCHAGUZI BILA BALAA Program sponsored by The European Union @EUinKenya and Search For Common Ground @SFCG_Kenya at the LICODEP Hall.

International Women's Day

We @Hisiacbo partnered with @swahilipothub and Dreams to commemorate #IWD2023 with the students of Vyemani Primary School. We had various talks with the young women ranging from self awareness, Sexual Reproductive Health and Rights (SRHR) and leadership.

Arising issues facing Youth

We engaged the Bata Point Youth Maskani, Bofu ward, Likoni in an open forum/ talk tackling post election related issues that the youths played a factor in their occurrence. We addressed several issues including the mediation, peaceful coexistence within them and the community

Hisia Youth Guiding and Counselling C.B.O is a dedicated non-profit organization, deeply rooted in the Likoni sub-county community and making a positive impact across Mombasa. Legally registered with the Ministry of Labor and Social Protection under the Department of Social Development, Hisia actively engages in guiding and counseling youth. Our mission is to empower and support the youth of Likoni, fostering personal growth, and making a lasting difference in their lives.
