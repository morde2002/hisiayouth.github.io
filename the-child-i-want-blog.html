'THE CHILD I WANT' Initiative: Nurturing Tomorrow's Leaders with Us

  • Feb 3, 2024
  • 3 Comments

Greetings from Hisia Youth Guiding and Counselling C.B.O! We are excited to share our latest initiative, 'THE CHILD I WANT,' a transformative project aimed at shaping the future generation of youth in our society.

Your journey with Hisia Youth C.B.O is more than a commitment; it's a shared endeavor to create lasting impact in the lives of individuals and the community. Together, let's build a future where every youth's potential is unlocked, and no one is left behind.

Empowering Young, Single Mothers for Lasting Impact

With a vision to create positive change, 'THE CHILD I WANT' focuses on empowering at least three hundred young, single mothers in Mombasa. Our long-term commitment involves providing comprehensive support to ensure the proper upbringing of their children and foster transformative change for a brighter tomorrow.

Comprehensive Support Ecosystem

At Hisia, we recognize the unique challenges faced by young, single mothers. Through 'THE CHILD I WANT,' we offer a comprehensive support ecosystem that includes educational resources, healthcare assistance, and emotional well-being programs. By equipping these mothers with essential parenting skills, we aim to create nurturing environments that promote holistic child development. Community plays a vital role in our initiative. We believe that by fostering collaboration and understanding, we can break down societal stigmas surrounding single motherhood. Together, we are building a supportive community that stands united in the face of challenges.

We invite you to join us in this transformative journey. With 'THE CHILD I WANT,' we are committed to empowering young, single mothers and nurturing the potential of the next generation. Together, we can create a brighter future and prove that we are #bettertogether.

Hisia Youth Guiding and Counselling C.B.O is a dedicated non-profit organization, deeply rooted in the Likoni sub-county community and making a positive impact across Mombasa. Legally registered with the Ministry of Labor and Social Protection under the Department of Social Development, Hisia actively engages in guiding and counseling youth. Our mission is to empower and support the youth of Likoni, fostering personal growth, and making a lasting difference in their lives.
