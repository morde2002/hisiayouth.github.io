About Us

A Champion in Mental Health, GBV, SRHR, and Substance Abuse Counseling

Welcome to Hisia Youth Guiding and Counselling C.B.O, a beacon of hope and empowerment in the heart of Likoni sub-county, Mombasa. As a non-profit community-based organization, we are passionately dedicated to guiding and counseling the youth, fostering personal development, and making a positive impact on our community. At Hisia, we believe in the transformative power of guidance and support, aiming to uplift and empower the youth to navigate life's challenges with resilience and purpose.

Our commitment extends to fostering mental health awareness, addressing gender-based violence (GBV), promoting sexual and reproductive health and rights (SRHR), and combating drug and substance abuse within the community. Through dedicated efforts and compassionate guidance, we strive to create a resilient, informed, and empowered community that thrives in overall well-being. Join us as we pave the way for positive change, one counseling session at a time.

Hisia Youth Guiding and Counselling C.B.O is a dedicated non-profit organization, deeply rooted in the Likoni sub-county community and making a positive impact across Mombasa. Legally registered with the Ministry of Labor and Social Protection under the Department of Social Development, Hisia actively engages in guiding and counseling youth. Our mission is to empower and support the youth of Likoni, fostering personal growth, and making a lasting difference in their lives.
