Commemorating Menstrual Hygiene

  • Feb 3, 2024
  • 0 Comments

On this day of commemoration, we stand united in our commitment to creating awareness about menstrual health and providing the necessary resources for young girls and women in Likoni and beyond. Our initiative focuses on education, accessibility to hygiene products, and fostering an open dialogue to eliminate stigma surrounding menstruation. In collaboration with local partners and volunteers, we conduct workshops and awareness campaigns to educate communities on the importance of proper menstrual hygiene practices. By breaking the silence and dispelling myths, we aim to empower young girls to embrace their menstrual health confidently.

Menstrual hygiene is not just a biological process; it's a crucial aspect of women's health and well-being. At Hisia Youth Guiding and Counselling C.B.O, we recognize the significance of menstrual hygiene in empowering women and breaking societal taboos.

In addition to educational programs, Hisia C.B.O actively works towards providing sustainable solutions, ensuring that hygiene products are accessible to all. We believe that every woman and girl deserves the right to manage their menstruation in a dignified and healthy way. As we commemorate Menstrual Hygiene Day, let's join hands to advocate for a world where menstruation is not a barrier but a natural and celebrated part of every woman's life. Together, we can break the silence, challenge norms, and create an inclusive society where every woman feels supported and empowered.

Join us in celebrating menstrual hygiene, breaking taboos, and fostering a future where no woman or girl is held back by her biology.

Hisia Youth Guiding and Counselling C.B.O is a dedicated non-profit organization, deeply rooted in the Likoni sub-county community and making a positive impact across Mombasa. Legally registered with the Ministry of Labor and Social Protection under the Department of Social Development, Hisia actively engages in guiding and counseling youth. Our mission is to empower and support the youth of Likoni, fostering personal growth, and making a lasting difference in their lives.
