Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn about our mission, involvement in community issues, activities, contributions to the well-being of the community, and how individuals can participate in our initiatives. Explore the core aspects of Hisia's work, and find out how you can be a part of the positive change we aim to bring to Likoni and beyond.

1. What is the mission of Hisia Youth Guiding & Counselling C.B.O?

Hisia Youth C.B.O is dedicated to guiding and counseling youth in Likoni and other parts of Mombasa, aiming to empower them with essential life skills and foster responsible citizenship.

2. How is Hisia C.B.O involved in preventing and countering violent extremism?

Hisia actively participates in community initiatives, as evidenced by our program director's representation in the Mombasa County Action Plan meeting, addressing political aspects and building a constituency against violent extremism.

3. What activities does Hisia undertake to achieve its objectives?

Hisia focuses on providing guidance and counseling on social issues, promoting discipline in schools, mentoring youth through career guidance, and empowering members through income-generating projects.

4. How does Hisia contribute to the well-being of the community?

Hisia is a not-for-profit organization with a legal registration, actively engaging in community-based initiatives to address social issues, improve education, and empower youth for a healthier society.

5. Can individuals contribute or get involved with Hisia's initiatives?

Yes, Hisia welcomes contributions and involvement from individuals who share our vision. Whether through donations, volunteering, or collaboration, we believe in creating positive change together.

Hisia Youth Guiding and Counselling C.B.O is a dedicated non-profit organization, deeply rooted in the Likoni sub-county community and making a positive impact across Mombasa. Legally registered with the Ministry of Labor and Social Protection under the Department of Social Development, Hisia actively engages in guiding and counseling youth. Our mission is to empower and support the youth of Likoni, fostering personal growth, and making a lasting difference in their lives.
