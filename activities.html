Our Activities

What we do

Hisia Youth Guiding and Counselling C.B.O is a not-for-profit community-based organization with active social footprint in Likoni sub-county. Hisia is legally registered with the ministry of labor and social protection under the department of social development. It aims at guiding youth in likoni and other parts of Mombasa

Our program director represented us in the Mombasa County Action Plan for preventing and countering violent extremism meeting. They tackled the political pillar by brainstorming how they could build a critical constituency opinion and political leaders who are active PVCE.





Uchaguzi Bila Balaa Program

We hosted local administrators, youth leaders and social media personnel from Likoni Subcounty for the Inception meeting of the UCHAGUZI BILA BALAA Program sponsored by The European Union @EUinKenya and Search For Common Ground @SFCG_Kenya at the LICODEP Hall. #UCHAGUZIBILABALAA.

International Women's Day

We, @Hisiacbo partnered with @swahilipothub and Dreams to commemorate #IWD2023 with the students of Vyemani Primary School. We had various talks with the young women ranging from self awareness, Sexual Reproductive Health and Rights (SRHR) and leadership.

Arising issues facing Youth

We engaged the Bata Point Youth Maskani, Bofu ward, Likoni in an open forum/ talk tackling post election related issues that the youths played a factor in their occurrence. We addressed several issues including the mediation, peaceful coexistence within them and the community
