Our Activities

In pursuit of our mission, we actively engage in various activities aimed at positively impacting the community. As a registered not-for-profit organization, we operate with a focus on guiding and mentoring the youth in Likoni and beyond. A notable initiative involves participation in the Mombasa County Action Plan, specifically addressing the prevention and countering of violent extremism. The program director, representing us, actively contributed to discussions on the political pillar, exploring strategies to build critical constituency opinions and involve political leaders in countering violent extremism efforts. Through such proactive engagement, we strive to create a lasting impact on the social landscape, fostering a community where youth are empowered and guided toward a brighter future.

  • Mission

    To impart youth with knowledge on the importance of education and campaign against the dangers of peer pressure and sexual immorality.

  • Vision

    A prosperous society where youths have acquired academic, career and social life skills hence managing their lives in a healthy responsible manner as productive citizens who respect themselves and others.

Creating Awareness Through Peace

At Hisia Youth Guiding and Counselling C.B.O, we believe in the transformative power of awareness to cultivate peace within communities. Our initiatives are centered around enlightening individuals on the significance of peaceful coexistence, promoting dialogue, and fostering understanding. Through workshops, seminars, and community outreach programs, we strive to create an environment where individuals are well-informed about the impacts of conflicts and the importance of building harmonious relationships.

Our commitment extends beyond conventional awareness campaigns; we actively engage with local leaders, educators, and influencers to amplify the message of peace. By partnering with schools, community organizations, and governmental bodies, we aim to reach a wider audience and instill the values of tolerance, empathy, and conflict resolution.

In collaboration with our program directors, we organize events that address various facets of peace-building, from interpersonal relationships to community harmony. Through these efforts, we aspire to contribute to the development of a society where individuals are not only aware of the benefits of peace but actively participate in its cultivation, fostering a culture of understanding and cooperation.

Uchaguzi Bila Balaa Program

We are proud to launch our "Uchaguzi Bila Balaa" program, a Swahili phrase translating to "Election Without Chaos." This initiative is designed to address the unique challenges and tensions that often accompany electoral processes. Recognizing the vital role elections play in shaping the future of our communities, we aim to promote peaceful and informed participation.

The "Uchaguzi Bila Balaa" program encompasses various components aimed at educating and engaging the public. Through targeted awareness campaigns, we seek to inform citizens about their voting rights, the electoral process, and the importance of civic responsibility. We organize workshops and seminars to facilitate constructive dialogue, encouraging citizens to express their opinions in a peaceful and respectful manner.

Our commitment to a peaceful electoral process extends to monitoring and reporting any potential issues that may arise. Through partnerships with relevant organizations, we work towards ensuring that elections are conducted smoothly, fostering an atmosphere of trust and confidence in the democratic process. The "Uchaguzi Bila Balaa" program reflects our dedication to building resilient communities that actively participate in shaping their democratic future.

Targeting Young and single Mothers

We understand the unique challenges faced by young and single mothers, and we are committed to providing support and empowerment to enhance their well-being. Our program specifically targets this demographic, recognizing the importance of fostering a nurturing environment for both mothers and their children.

The mentorship programs connect these mothers with experienced individuals who can offer valuable insights and advice. This supportive network encourages a sense of community and shared experiences, helping young mothers feel less isolated and more empowered to overcome challenges. By targeting young and single mothers, our organization aims to break down societal stigmas and create a supportive community that uplifts and empowers these resilient individuals. Through this program, we aspire to contribute to the holistic well-being and success of both mothers and their children.

Menstrual Hygiene

Through awareness campaigns, educational workshops, and the distribution of menstrual hygiene products, we aim to eliminate the stigma associated with menstruation. We believe that open conversations and education play a crucial role in dispelling myths and misconceptions surrounding this natural biological process.

Our educational workshops provide information on menstrual hygiene practices, emphasizing the importance of using safe and sustainable menstrual products. We address topics such as proper disposal methods, hygiene routines, and the significance of maintaining good menstrual health.

In collaboration with local communities, schools, and healthcare providers, we strive to make menstrual hygiene products accessible to women and girls who may face financial barriers. By ensuring the availability of affordable and eco-friendly options, we contribute to creating a more inclusive and supportive environment for women of all ages.

Hisia Youth Guiding and Counselling C.B.O is a dedicated non-profit organization, deeply rooted in the Likoni sub-county community and making a positive impact across Mombasa. Legally registered with the Ministry of Labor and Social Protection under the Department of Social Development, Hisia actively engages in guiding and counseling youth. Our mission is to empower and support the youth of Likoni, fostering personal growth, and making a lasting difference in their lives.
