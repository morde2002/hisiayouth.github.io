The Inception

Embarking on our journey, Hisia Youth Guiding and Counselling C.B.O originated with a modest team, limited resources, and an ambitious objective: to guide and empower the youth in Likoni and beyond. Our visionary founders, Salma Ali Abdallah and Maryam Ngare, recognized the need for a paradigm shift in addressing the challenges faced by the youth. Just as John Marks revolutionized conflict resolution during the Cold War, our founders aim to redefine the narrative, emphasizing collective growth and empowerment rather than divisive struggles. As Hisia takes its first steps, the spirit of constructive change is at the core of our foundation.

Current Initiatives

At present, Hisia Youth Guiding and Counselling C.B.O actively engages with the community, offering guidance and support to the youth in Likoni and other areas of Mombasa. Through various programs, we address crucial aspects of mental health, gender-based violence, sexual and reproductive health, and substance abuse. Our ongoing efforts aim to create a positive impact on the lives of the youth, empowering them with the necessary skills to navigate the challenges they face.

Looking ahead, Hisia envisions expanding its reach and influence, becoming a catalyst for positive change on a broader scale. Our future goals include implementing comprehensive educational campaigns, establishing more counseling centers, and forging partnerships with like-minded organizations. We aspire to foster a society where every youth is equipped with the tools to lead a healthy, responsible life. With a commitment to continuous growth, Hisia aims to make enduring contributions to the well-being of the community and beyond.

Hisia Youth Guiding and Counselling C.B.O is a dedicated non-profit organization, deeply rooted in the Likoni sub-county community and making a positive impact across Mombasa. Legally registered with the Ministry of Labor and Social Protection under the Department of Social Development, Hisia actively engages in guiding and counseling youth. Our mission is to empower and support the youth of Likoni, fostering personal growth, and making a lasting difference in their lives.
